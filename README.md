# GenKeyUtil
Android application which generates the key to unlocking crs retailstore locked registers. 
<p>Key Algorithm:</p>
First 5 digits | key  | Last Digits
<p>@ | 0 | P</p>
<p>A | 1 | Q</p>
<p>B | 2 | R</p>
<p>C | 3 | S</p>
<p>D | 4 | T</p>
<p>E | 5 | U</p>
<p>F | 6 | V</p>
<p>G | 7 | W</p>
<p>H | 8 | X</p>
<p>I | 9 | Y</p>
